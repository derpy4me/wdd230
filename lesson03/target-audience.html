Target Audience

The chamber of commerce is for professionals and businesses to come together and network. As such the target of this website will be for businesses who are looking for an opportunity to grow in their local community. This website will display the benefits of being a member of the Chamber of commerce and help for businesses decide if it will be beneficial.

Scenarios

The scenarios for people visiting this website will run along the lines of:
How can I get my business to grow?
Are there other's with more experience who could help me?
Can the Chamber of Commerce help me?

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to show upcoming meetings and events that will benefit the commerce community in Buffalo, Wyoming. The site will share information on what a Chamber of Commerce responsibilities are and how they are accomplished. It will be also a wonderful opportunity to promote local businesses and give them support. Members of the chamber of commerce can also be introduced to give more of a feeling of familiarity. The entire value of this site is to increase cohesion among the community through communications of events and their purpose.
